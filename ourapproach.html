Our Approach

To provide an alternate livelihood to fisherwomen / Women SHG Groupfamilies in the coastal districts at least 150+ families to create avenues for additional income to Coastal fishers/fisherwomen. The production of seaweed has manifold industrial benefits. Out of approximately 700 species of marine algae found in both intertidal and deep-sea glasses of water on the Indian Coast. Nearly 60 SPS are commercially important. The seaweed industry in India is mainly a cottage industry and is based only on the natural stock of Agar yielding red seaweeds and Alginyielding brown seaweeds.
