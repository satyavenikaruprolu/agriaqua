Coral Nurseries

Coral reefs are the most diverse marine ecosystems on the planet, supporting 25% of all marine life. They are also the most threatened marine ecosystem, with 50% of the world’s reefs already lost and another 40% predicted to be lost by 2050. The loss of coral reefs is a global crisis that will have devastating impacts on the world’s oceans and the billions of people who depend on them.
