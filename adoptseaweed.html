Adopt Seaweed

Seaweed cultivation, when adopted with the right strategy and on a large scale, offers a multifaceted solution to mitigate biodiversity loss, excess nitrogen, ocean acidification, climate change, and land use conflicts. Seaweeds are not only nutritious but also environmentally friendly, serving as a valuable biomass source for food, health products, and animal feed. Additionally, they play a vital role in reducing the environmental impact of other industries and fostering collaboration for more sustainable practices and resource utilization.

Coral Nurseries

Coral reefs are the most diverse marine ecosystems on the planet, supporting 25% of all marine life. They are also the most threatened marine ecosystem, with 50% of the world’s reefs already lost and another 40% predicted to be lost by 2050. The loss of coral reefs is a global crisis that will have devastating impacts on the world’s oceans and the billions of people who depend on them.
